Web Development

In today's digital world, your website is often the first impression people have of your business, service, or brand. I'm here to make sure it's a good one. I specialize in crafting clean, modern, and mobile-friendly websites that don't just look great—but work great too.

As the founder of HTML by Design, I approach every project with a hands-on mindset. Whether you're looking to build your first website, give an old one a new lease on life, or need someone to handle the technical side of getting your site online—I've got your back.

What I Do:

  • Custom Website Design: Hand-coded websites using HTML and CSS, tailored to your style, brand, and needs. No cookie-cutter templates—just bespoke design.
  • Responsive Layouts: Your website will look sharp and function smoothly on desktops, tablets, and mobiles. A seamless experience no matter what screen your users are on.
  • Fast Loading Speeds: Optimized code and clean structure mean your site loads fast. No waiting around, no bloated pages—just smooth performance.
  • Simple Navigation: I build sites that are easy to use and intuitive to navigate. Visitors will find what they’re looking for without frustration or confusion.
  • Search Engine Ready: My builds are structured for SEO, giving your site the best chance to be seen on Google and other search engines.
  • Accessibility Focus: Websites that everyone can use, including users with disabilities or slower internet connections.

My Process:

  1. Discovery: We’ll talk about what you need, who your site is for, and what goals you have in mind.
  2. Design Mockup: I can provide a visual layout of the site (using Canva or Figma) so you can approve the look before I touch code.
  3. Build: This is where the magic happens. I bring the design to life using clean, organized code that’s easy to manage and maintain.
  4. Launch & Support: Once your site is ready, I’ll help with hosting setup, domain linking, and any ongoing support you might need after launch.
