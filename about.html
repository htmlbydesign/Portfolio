Who I Am

Hi, I'm Mark — a hands-on, self-taught web developer and tech support specialist based in the UK. I didn’t come up through a fancy degree or Silicon Valley startup. I came up the hard way — learning on the job, staying up late breaking things, fixing them again, and figuring it all out piece by piece. I’m a believer in real experience, self-education, and putting your skills to the test in the real world.

I started HTML by Design to bring no-nonsense, honest tech services to people who just want stuff to work. Whether it’s a slick new website or helping someone fix their Wi-Fi, I approach every job with the same focus: do it right, explain it clearly, and leave the client better off than when I arrived.

Before I stepped into web dev and tech, I worked with my hands in tough jobs. I know what it’s like to graft hard and get little in return. That’s why I put so much pride into what I do now — because I’m not just building websites or fixing PCs, I’m building a better life for myself and a better experience for my clients.

What Drives Me

  • Helping people: Whether it’s a business owner or a mate struggling with a laptop, I love being the guy who gets things working again.
  • Creative problem-solving: No two jobs are the same. I like working out clever, practical solutions to weird tech problems.
  • Learning every day: Tech never stops changing, and I never stop learning. It keeps things exciting and sharp.

What You Get With Me

You’re not hiring a faceless company. You’re working with someone who listens, explains things clearly, and treats your project like it matters — because it does. I’m reliable, flexible, and I won’t blind you with buzzwords or try to sell you stuff you don’t need.

If you want real service from someone who gives a damn, you’ve found the right guy.
