Software & Hardware Upgrades

Got an old laptop or sluggish PC? I bring new life into tired machines — whether it’s a slow startup, poor performance, or just outdated software. I offer practical upgrades and full servicing to help your tech run like new.

What I Can Do:

  • SSD Installation: Replace your slow hard drive with a fast solid-state drive (SSD) for a massive speed boost.
  • RAM Upgrades: Upgrade your memory to handle more apps, smoother multitasking, and faster load times.
  • Driver & Firmware Updates: I’ll track down the latest drivers and install them to get everything running smoothly.
  • Fresh OS Install: Clean Windows installs to clear bloatware and fix persistent errors.
  • Software Cleanup: Remove junk apps, fix broken software, and optimize your settings for performance.
  • Thermal Paste + Cleanout: For desktop PCs — I’ll clean out dust, reapply thermal paste, and lower temps.

Whether it’s a student laptop, a family desktop, or your personal workhorse — I’ll make sure it’s working like it should. No upsell, no gimmicks — just honest upgrades that make a real difference.
