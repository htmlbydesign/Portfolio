Frequently Asked Questions

How much do you charge for a basic website?

Basic sites start from £150 for a 1–3 page build. Prices can vary depending on features and complexity.

How long does it take to build a site?

Most basic sites are completed within 7–10 days, but it depends on content readiness and project size.

Do you help with domains and hosting?

Absolutely. I offer full hosting setup support including domain registration, DNS linking, and file uploads.

Can you fix my slow laptop?

Yes! I offer remote tech support and hardware upgrade services like SSD installs, RAM upgrades, and cleanups.

Do I need to be tech-savvy to work with you?

Not at all. I keep things jargon-free and walk you through anything you’re unsure about.

Is support included after a job is finished?

Yes. I provide post-launch support and advice, and I’m available for future updates or ongoing help.
