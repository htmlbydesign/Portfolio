Whether you're starting from scratch or need help moving your existing site to a new host, I provide clear, step-by-step support. I handle the tech bits and walk you through everything in plain English.

What I Offer:

  • Domain Registration Assistance: Help choosing and registering your domain.

  • FTP & File Uploads: I’ll upload your site and ensure it’s live and functional.
  • Email Setup (Optional): I can help set up your custom email addresses too.
